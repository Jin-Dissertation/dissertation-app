Recorded reflection prompts:

  1. In your own words, how do you understand the purpose of this activity and what it is intended to help educators accomplish?
  2. To what extent do you see yourself or other educators being willing to use an approach like this when creating exam questions?
  3. Thinking about your own teaching context, how practical would it be to incorporate a process like this into your workflow?
  4. After completing today's session, how do you evaluate the usefulness of this approach for generating multiple-choice questions?

A single, unscripted take is preferred. Please do not feel the need to prepare, re-record, or edit your response.

Training tip Click End Session when you are finished practicing.

You do not need to produce perfect questions — the goal of this activity is simply to practice using the app. Your responses will be saved when you end the session.
