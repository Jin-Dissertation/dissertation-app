              ⓘTraining tip
              
The starter prompt is now on your clipboard. Switch to your AI product and paste it into the message box (Ctrl+V on Windows/Chromebook, or ⌘+V on Mac). The Clipboard panel on the right shows what you last copied. Use the icons at the top of the panel to switch between a preview of the current copy and a history of everything you've copied this session (tap and hold in the text field, then tap Paste). After the AI responds, come back here and proceed down.

              
Each prompt you copy stays on your clipboard until the next one replaces it. You can always re-copy a button if you need it again.
