Study Information

Information for non-participants who want to learn more about the study connected to this tool.

If you meet the eligibility criteria and wish to express interest in participating, you may complete the screening form here.

Who is eligible for the study?

This study is open to actively working prelicensure nurse educators whose institutions have provided them with institutional access to one of the following AI products: ChatGPT, Gemini, or Copilot.

Important note

You do not need to be enthusiastic about AI to participate. I am specifically seeking a range of perspectives, including those of educators who feel uncertain about AI, cautious about it, skeptical of it, or not enthusiastic about using it.

What is this study about?

This study explores how prelicensure nurse educators experience the use of AI for multiple choice question writing.

What would participation involve?

If you qualify and choose to participate, participation would involve two interviews and completion of a training module.

Additional details about the study procedures and time commitment would be provided before you decide whether to participate.
